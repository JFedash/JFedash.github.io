Sales Tax Recovery with Stratos (August 2024)

Identified tax overpayments and supported audit processes using Excel and SAP during a system transition at Teva Pharmaceuticals.

Problem/Goal

During Teva’s transition from Oracle to SAP, some transactional data was improperly reported or left unaccounted for. Our goal was to identify overpaid sales tax on legacy transactions and assist with audit responses to the CRA and Revenue Quebec.

My Role

I worked with both Teva’s internal IT team and the Stratos Solutions recovery team to investigate and identify tax overpayments. My responsibilities focused on data extraction, validation, reconciliation, and reporting.

Process

We extracted relevant transactional data from SAP and reformatted it according to audit and recovery standards. I used Excel to clean, organize, and reconcile data, frequently identifying mismatches in tax values or missing entries. When discrepancies appeared, I used various SAP navigation methods to trace and retrieve missing transactions.

Throughout the process, we collaborated closely with Stratos Solutions, sending regular reports and adjusting our output based on their feedback and audit requirements.

Challenges and Solutions

There was no predefined workflow, and we regularly encountered issues such as incomplete fields, mismatched totals, or improperly exported data. I used logical cross-checking in Excel and iterative testing in SAP to track down inconsistencies and fill in the gaps. I had to think critically and adapt quickly to ensure the recovery files were accurate and complete.

Outcome

I contributed to identifying C$401,000 in tax overpayments across legacy transactions, enabling Teva to recover significant amounts of GST, HST, and QST. I also supported the preparation of data packages for audit requests from the Canada Revenue Agency and Revenue Quebec, ensuring compliance and transparency throughout the process.

This experience demonstrated my ability to apply technical tools and analytical thinking in a real-world enterprise environment.

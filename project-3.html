Sales Tax Recovery with Stratos (August 2024)

Identified tax overpayments and supported audit processes using Excel and SAP during Teva Pharmaceuticals’ transition to a new ERP system.

Overview

During a sales tax recovery engagement at Teva Pharmaceuticals, I worked with Stratos Solutions to identify over C$400,000 in overpaid GST, HST, and QST. My role involved extracting and reconciling transactional data from SAP, resolving discrepancies, and preparing audit-ready documentation. The project provided hands-on experience with data validation, financial compliance, and enterprise tools such as Excel and SAP.

Problem/Goal

Teva was transitioning from Oracle to SAP, and discrepancies in historical tax reporting surfaced during the data migration. Our objective was to identify overpayments across legacy transactions and assist in audit responses to the Canada Revenue Agency and Revenue Quebec.

My Role

I served as a liaison between Teva’s IT and Stratos Solutions’ recovery team, focusing on data analysis and reconciliation. My key tasks included retrieving transactional data, cleaning and validating entries, identifying overpayments, and preparing structured reports to support tax recovery efforts.

Process

Data Retrieval and Cleaning
I extracted relevant financial records from SAP and formatted them to align with audit and recovery standards. Using Excel, I cleaned, organized, and reconciled data across multiple sources, identifying gaps or inconsistencies in tax values.

Issue Resolution
When discrepancies arose, I used logical testing and cross-checks in Excel to trace problems. I also navigated SAP to locate missing or misclassified transactions, ensuring completeness and accuracy in our recovery files.

Collaboration and Reporting
Throughout the project, I worked closely with Stratos Solutions to tailor our deliverables to audit expectations. Regular feedback loops helped refine our output and ensure alignment with CRA and Revenue Quebec requirements.

Challenges and Solutions

The project lacked a structured workflow, and data irregularities were common. I addressed these issues by developing my own logic-driven reconciliation processes and applying problem-solving techniques in both Excel and SAP. My adaptability and attention to detail ensured high-quality results despite incomplete or inconsistent data.

Outcome

I helped identify over C$401,000 in recoverable sales tax and supported the preparation of audit-ready data packages. The project strengthened Teva’s compliance posture and resulted in a successful submission to tax authorities. This experience enhanced my technical capabilities and demonstrated my ability to contribute in high-stakes, enterprise-level environments.
